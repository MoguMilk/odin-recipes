Baked Salmon Recipe

Baked Salmon Description

Baked Salmon is a delicious dinner dish rich is fatty acids salmon can be good for your heart and overall health

Baked Salmon ingredients:

  • Salmon Fillets
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Cracked Black Pepper
  • Minced Garlic
  • Italian Herb Seasoning Blend
  • Lemon

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees grease the pan and put the salmon fillets on the baking sheet while seasoning them
  2. Stir the the olive oil, garlic, herbs, and 1/2 of the lemon juice, spoon the sauce over salmon make sure to get the dry spots too
  3. Bake the salmon in the oven for 12-15 minutes until the salmon looks opaque and flaky
  4. Garnish with fresh thyme or parsley to your liking and serve
