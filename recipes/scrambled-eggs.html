Scrambled Eggs Recipe

Scrambled Eggs description

Scrambled eggs are a delicious breakfast item that we normally have in the morning rich and protein and flavor it's most peoples go-to

Scrambled egg ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Milk,plant milk, or water
  • Extra-virgin olive oil or butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. Beat rhe eggs and add milk or water mix once added
  2. Gently preheat the pan brush skillet with olive oil or melt a small portion of butter
  3. Continue cooking over a medium-low heat folding anf stirring the eggs every few seconds
  4. Take off pan when satisfied and serve
