Dragon Roll Sushi Recipe

Dragon roll recipe Description

Dragon rolls are a delicious variation of sushi rich is flavor and nutrition

Dragon roll ingredients:

  • 1 Japanese or Persian cucumber
  • 2 avocados
  • 1/2 lemon
  • 2 sheets nori/dried laver seaweed
  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 8 pieces shrimp tempura
  • 2 Tbsp tobiko
  • unagi

For toppings:

  • Homemade spicy Mayo
  • Unagi Sauce
  • Toasted black sesame seeds

Steps:

  1. Gather all the ingredients
  2. Cut the cucumber into quarters
  3. Cut the avocado in thin slices
  4. Line the bamboo mat with plastic wrap, place half nori sheet on top, spread tezu and spread 1/2 rice over nori sheet
  5. FFlip up nori sheet/Place shrimp tempura, cucumber, and tobiko at the bottom
  6. From the bottom of the bamboo sheet start rolling nori sheet firmly and tightly until bottom edge reaches nori sheet
  7. Place bamboo mat over the top and tightly squeeze the roll
  8. Remove the bamboo mat and gently place the avocado slices on top using the knife
  9. Re-place the plastic wrap over the roll, put the bamboo wrap back on and gently and tightly squeeze
  10. Cut the roll through the plastic wrap into 8 pieces
  11. Put tobiko on each pieces of sushi and drizzle spicy mayo as well as sprinkle black sesame seeds on top
